Our Mission

We're building practical, lab-grounded detection tools that protect forage quality and guide efficient weed control. Invasive annual grasses like ventenata, medusahead, and cheatgrass threaten hay quality, pasture productivity, and ecosystem health across the Western U.S.

Early detection is the key to cost-effective management, but it's hard. These weeds often go unnoticed until infestations are severe. We're changing that with spectral detection tools that find contamination early‚Äîwhen it's still manageable.
