Why detection is hard

Plant targets vary by medium, moisture, and region.

Subtle signatures

Spectral signals are faint when plants are mixed with other material or environmental noise.

Field variability

Different growth stages and moisture levels can shift spectra in unpredictable ways.

Slow confirmation

Lab methods like DNA testing can be accurate but are not practical for real-time decisions.

Our solution approach

Targeted calibration with repeatable field workflows.

Controlled capture

  • Close-range geometry and internal illumination
  • Smartphone-based workflow for rapid results
  • Designed for mixed media and direct plant scans
